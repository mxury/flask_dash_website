About me

Hi I'm Miki. I'm deeply excited about data science and machine learning due to their wide applicability and ability to answer questions which are not answerable with only pen & paper. I have used a variety of different online courses and textbooks to teach myself machine learning, and I have been applying that knowledge to a myriad of personal projects which can be found here.

Quite recently I finished a Theoretical Physics PhD at University College London. My research investigated fundamental theories of materials, specifically solid materials in which the collective behaviour of electrons gives rise to new and exotic phases of matter, like superconductivity or anti-ferromagnetism. To find out more about my PhD research please visit the Publications page.
